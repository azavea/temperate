What is this?

Climate Change Data for your Community

Climate gives you access to more data, identifies major risks to your region, assists you in anticipating the impacts of climate change on your community, and lets you come away with a robust first draft of an adaptation plan. It goes beyond the presentation of generic information by tailoring the projected risk factors to your specific city or town.

Unlike other applications that simply give you the data and expect you to know how to translate that into a plan, as a registered Climate member, you will have access to experts to help you find the right information, understand the impacts of climate change on your community, and create an adaptation plan for you to share with other stakeholders.

Think through the impacts

Digesting the data is just the first step in the process. Climate will guide you through the next steps, which include conducting a vulnerability assessment and planning for action.

Get expert guidance

As a registered member, you’ll have on-demand access to a adaptation planner at ICLEI. With more than 25 years of experience helping local governments adopt sustainable practices, ICLEI staff will assist you in building your adaptation plan.
